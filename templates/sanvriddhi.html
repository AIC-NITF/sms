About Sanvriddhi

AIC-NITF is conducting a Startup Acceleration Program during the months of May-June for the Odisha based Startups at Pre-Market or Early Traction levels of growth.The Acceleration program is designed to give the participating startups a complete strategic outlook towards their business proposal and prepare for the future challenges pertaining to their respective fields of operation. The program will also be beneficial to those startups who are looking forward to pivot their ideas or expand into new avenues of business.
The program is being partnered by Lemon School of Entrepreneurship who shall be the Knowledge Partner for the pedagogical learning sessions and assessment evaluation methodology. The entire program is designed to train and develop foundational entrepreneurial skills that shall enable the startups to develop capabilities for taking up the challenging journey of Entrepreneurship and make them market and investment ready.

Benefits of the Programme

  • Idea Validation
  • Collaboration Activity
  • Problem Solution Modeling
  • Business Modeling
  • Business Planning and Funding
  • Managing Human Resource
  • Managing Account & Bookkeeping
  • Understanding Investor Empathy
  • Presentation Skills
  • IPR and Legal Compliances
  • Pitch Deck Presentation
  • Dedicated Mentorship
  • Market Access
